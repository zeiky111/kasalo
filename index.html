About Us

Welcome to Kasalo!

Kasalo brings people together through authentic Filipino food made with care and tradition. Inspired by the Filipino word "kasalo," meaning someone you share a meal with, we serve comforting Filipino favorites made with quality ingredients perfect for gatherings, celebrations, or everyday meals.

Conveniently located at PurePrep Commissary Kitchen (#301 20381 62 Ave, Langley, BC, V3A 5E6), we're dedicated to bringing the warmth of Filipino hospitality to your table. Whether it's a large catering order or a smaller meal through our online delivery services, we prepare every dish with love and authenticity.
