## 📤 Deployment Steps

### Option 1: Traditional Web Hosting (Recommended)

1. **Purchase Hosting**
   - Choose a hosting plan (Shared hosting is fine to start)
   - Cost: Usually $5-15 CAD/month

2. **Upload Website Files**
   Upload these files to your hosting via cPanel or FTP:
   - `index.html`
   - `styles.css`
   - `script.js`
   - `83355588-a15c-4bab-9118-a0e02725d9d1.jpg` (logo)

3. **Connect Domain**
   - Log into your domain registrar (where you bought kasalo.ca)
   - Update nameservers or DNS records to point to your hosting
   - Your hosting provider will give you specific instructions

4. **Wait for Propagation**
   - DNS changes take 24-48 hours to fully propagate
   - Your website will then be live at kasalo.ca

## ✏️ How to Update Content

### Update Menu Prices:
1. Open `index.html`
2. Find the "Menu Section" (around line 90)
3. Update package prices and items
4. Find "Gallery Section" for individual dish pricing

### Update Contact Information:
1. Open `index.html`
2. Search for "Contact Information" section
3. Update phone numbers, email, or address
4. Save and re-upload to hosting

### Update Social Media Links:
1. Open `index.html`
2. Find "Social Media Section"
3. Update Facebook, Instagram, TikTok links
4. Save and re-upload

### Change Brand Colors:
1. Open `styles.css`
2. Find `:root` section at top
3. Update color variables:
```css
--primary-gold: #D4AF37;
--primary-black: #1B1B1B;
--accent-green: #3A7A5E;
```
